================================================================================
SENIOR BACKEND ENGINEER
================================================================================

Company:    TechCo Inc.
Location:   San Francisco, CA (Hybrid - 2 days/week in office)
Department: Platform Engineering
Reports to: Engineering Manager

================================================================================
ABOUT THE COMPANY
================================================================================

TechCo is a fast-growing B2B SaaS platform helping enterprises manage their
digital workflows. We've raised $50M Series B and are expanding our
engineering team to build the next generation of our platform.

================================================================================
ABOUT THE ROLE
================================================================================

We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform team. You'll
be responsible for designing and implementing scalable microservices that
power our core product. This is a high-impact role where you'll work closely
with product, design, and other engineering teams.

================================================================================
RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

• Design and implement scalable microservices using Python and Go
• Lead technical design reviews and architecture discussions
• Mentor junior developers and contribute to hiring
• Collaborate with product managers to define technical requirements
• Ensure high availability and performance of critical systems
• Participate in on-call rotation (1 week per month)

================================================================================
REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

Must Have:
----------
• 5+ years of experience in backend development
• Strong proficiency in Python or Go
• Experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration
• Solid understanding of distributed systems and microservices
• Experience with SQL (PostgreSQL) and NoSQL (Redis, DynamoDB) databases
• Strong communication skills and ability to work in a team
• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience

Nice to Have:
-------------
• Experience with event streaming (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
• Knowledge of machine learning pipelines
• Experience with infrastructure as code (Terraform)
• Previous startup experience
• Contributions to open source projects
